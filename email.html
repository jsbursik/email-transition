Questions
What about my old email address? (If changed)
Don't worry about it.
All emails to your prior address will be forwarded to your new address. You can change your business cards whenever you want.
What about the emails on my Computer?
They will have to get transferred to the new account.
If you are a manager or in accounting, Jordan will come around to do this for you. Sales personnel will no longer use software (Thunderbird, Outlook, etc.), they will use the online portal instead.
What about the emails on my Phone?
Good news, you'll be able to set up the account yourself.
This goes for everybody. If you use an iPhone you can follow this guide. If you use an Android, there are too many mail apps for me to link guides to. Look up your app and how to add a Microsoft Exchange account. I will not help people who don't even try to use a guide. There are too many employees for me to do this for everyone.
Do I have to use the Microsoft Authenticator?
You have to use an Authenticator, it doesn't have to be Microsoft's.
The authenticator is a requirement of the FTC to protect customer information. If you have an authenticator you already use, click the option during your account setup to use a different authenticator. I suggest the Google Authenticator, it's super simple and used by a lot of other services.
